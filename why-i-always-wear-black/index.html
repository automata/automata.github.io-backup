Why I always wear black

automata
2017-04-29
life, style, wear

Don’t you want to wear something different? No.

Are you using it to hide your real shape? No, I don’t care.

Do you use the same t-shirt over and over again? No, I have more than 10 t-shirts.

Do you take a shower everyday? Yes, actually I do! :-)

Those are the questions random people on streets and believe, even my family, keep asking me. That’s why I decide to write this post. From now I can point people to this site when they ask. So, if you’re arriving here by this mean, congrats, this post is for you.

Having a signature look

Yep, signature look, it really exists and are quite common. You know people like Mark Zuckerberg or Steve Jobs developed their own. So I developed mine.

My look is quite simple:

OK… but why?!

There are many advantages in having a signature look, let me point some of them:

  • Time: I saved lots of time, believe me. Not only the time I used to spend every morning thinking about what to wear – and specially the time when I got pissed off because my favourite t-shirt was on the washing basket and I couldn’t wear it. Just think about the time I saved not having to look for new clothes on stores.

  • Freedom of mind: I don’t think about clothes anymore. I just peek one t-shirt, one pair of pants or shorts and I’m ready to go. I just freed my mind to more important details.

  • Style: I can’t really find a cloth I really enjoied before. To do that I would have to make mine own clothes. However I don’t have time to do that, so it’s so much easier to pick a color than to choose between gazillions of brands and models.

Cool… but why black?!

It’s my favorite color. That’s it. End of section. :-)
